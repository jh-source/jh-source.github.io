Research

I am particularly interested in enabling a more reliable and efficient power grid from a computer science perspective, mainly through learning and optimization methodologies. Currently, my research topics include:

1) Developing robust and efficient algorithms to manage uncertainties in power system operations and electric vehicle charging;

2) Designing effective energy forecasting algorithms for both system-level and residential-level applications.
